How to Use

  1. Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
  2. Point your camera at a QR code
  3. Position the QR code within the scanning frame
  4. The scanner will automatically detect and decode the QR code
  5. Click "Stop Scanner" to turn off the camera
  6. For best results, ensure adequate lighting and hold your device steady
